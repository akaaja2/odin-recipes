Steak

Description

Ipso etc

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Aromatic herbs
  • Steak

Steps

  1. Heat pan, melt butter
  2. Start timer, add Steak
  3. After 2 mins, add herbs
  4. After 1 more min, flip Steak
  5. Periodically spoon melted butter over Steak top
  6. After 3 more mins (six total), remove Steak from pan
  7. Rest for 1 min, cut off fat if desired, serve
  8. Steak. Done.
